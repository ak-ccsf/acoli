About

This is a software project created for CS 195 Software Engineering Practicum at City College of San Francisco in Fall 2021. The purpose of this site is to present a convenient way of viewing and comparing various quality of life metrics in cities across the world.

This site was created for educational purposes and utilizes crowd source information for the data found in this site. Quality of life metrics were all taken from numbeo.com. Photos from the "My Place" quiz are taken from each city's Wikipedia page.

In this site, our search function displays which cities are present in our database. You can choose to browse through the statistics for various quality of life metrics. For individual cities, choose "Quality of Life." To compare two cities, choose "City Compare." To input your criteria and find a best matching city, try our "My Place" quiz.
